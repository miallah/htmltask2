pink pasta

Pink pasta is a creamy, colorful dish made by mixing tomato sauce with cream, creating a soft pink sauce. It’s usually tossed with pasta, garlic, butter, and herbs, then topped with cheese for a rich and balanced flavor.

" INGREDIENS "
  • Pasta
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Heavy cream (Cooking cream)
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt & black pepper
  • Basil
  • Red pepper flakes

"STEPS"
  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat butter or olive oil and sauté the minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato paste or marinara sauce and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  4. Pour in the heavy cream and stir until the sauce turns pink and creamy.
  5. Season with salt, black pepper, and herbs (basil or oregano).
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss until evenly coated.
  7. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top and serve warm.
